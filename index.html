PRIVATE DINING

The Eddy is available to reserve as a private dining space for up to 32 people. Our space is filled with rich wood and vintage finishes throughout, exposed brick walls and a bar area. We are able to work accommodate seated dinners and cocktail parties up to 50 people. Please contact us for pricing and additional information at krisgoorhuis@gmail.com.

RESERVATIONS

Reservations for up to 6 people can be made by telephone (646-555-9884), e-mail krisgoorhuis@gmail.com, or below through Reservibot

PLEASE NOTE

For parties of 5 or more, a credit card is required to reserve the table; in case of cancellation within 24 hours of the reservation, a $25 per person fee will be charged.
